 1) project name: KeenKeeper
 
 2) description:
 KeenKeeper is a modern Friend list management web application designed to help users stay connected with the people who matter most . it allows users to keep track of friends, monitor contact frequency , view interaction timelines , and take a quick action like call, text . pr video call to stay in touch. the project building a smooth, organized, and user-friendly experience for managing friendships.

 3) technologies used:
    - next.js
    - react.js
    - javascript
    - tailwind css
    - react-icons
    - react-toastify
    - HTML

 4) Features:
   
   1. Friendship Analytics: 
   Get a visual overview of relationships activity though a clean analytics section that helps users understand their communication patterns and stay connected with friends.
   
    2. Interaction Timeline Tracking:
    Every call, text, or video interaction is recorded in a structured timeline,  making it easy to review past interactions and filter activities by type.

    3. Statistical Insights:
    Users can access detailed statistics about their interactions, such as the frequency of contact with each friend and the types of interactions they have had, helping them to maintain and strengthen their friendships.
